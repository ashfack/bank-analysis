Bank Analysis

From a bank transaction export, we want to generate insights from salary to salary or from first day of month to the last:
- Total salaries received.
- Total expenses.
- Total savings for the period.
- Total savings compared to your theoretical average salary (i.e., excluding on-call bonuses, exceptional increases, etc.).
- Number of expenses per period.
- Averages, with an option to exclude outliers (such as negative months or months with exceptional circumstances).
- Advanced mode that provides a detailed breakdown for each period by expense category, including both the total amount and the number of transactions per category.

Your personal data is not being stored, and it is processed locally here.
